Application Process

The application requirements for graduate admission for US are as follows:

  • Test score needed: GRE conducted by ETS (https://www.ets.org/)
  • English proficiency test: IELTS/TOEFL
  • Work experience
  • 10th 12th Marksheet
Application portal: There is no centralized application. You need to submit at the university portal.
